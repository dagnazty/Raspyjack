A CYBERPUNK MANIFESTO
by Christian As. Kirtchev (1997)

==============================================================================

We are the ELECTRONIC MINDS, a group of free-minded rebels. Cyberpunks.
We live in Cyberspace, we are everywhere, we know no boundaries.

We are those, the Different. Technological rats, swimming in the ocean of
information. We are the introverted little kid in the corner of the room.
We are the teenager nobody speaks to. We are the student hacking on his
laptop in the library. We are the grown-up in the dim-lit office.

We exist without nationality, skin color, or religious bias.

You wage wars, murder, cheat, lie to us, and try to make us believe it's
for our own good, yet we're the criminals.

Yes, we are criminals. Our crime is that of curiosity. Our crime is that
of judging people by what they say and think, not what they look like.
Our crime is that of outsmarting you, something that you will never
forgive us for.

We are the Cyberpunks. We are the ones who don't give a damn.
We are the technology. We are the future.
We are the last free rebels.

==============================================================================

The Cyberpunk is the truly free man. He lives by his own rules. He does
not conform to the norms of society. He is the last vestige of freedom
in a world of control.

Information wants to be free. We set it free.

Technology is our tool. Code is our weapon. Knowledge is our shield.
The network is our home.

We are everywhere and nowhere. We are the ghost in the machine.
We are the Cyberpunks.

Long live the Cyberpunks. Long live the free spirits.
